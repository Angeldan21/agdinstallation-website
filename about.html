About AGD Installation

A Hayward crew that treats your office like their own.

AGD Installation is a commercial furniture moving and installation company based in Hayward, California. We serve businesses across the Bay Area and the rest of the state — from single-room installs to multi-floor relocations.

Our story

Built by movers, for businesses.

AGD started as a small crew doing weekend jobs for local businesses that couldn't find a mover who'd show up on time. Twelve years later, we still pick up the phone, still do walkthroughs, and still have the same supervisors running the jobs.

We're not a van line. We don't do residential. Every person on our crew moves offices for a living — that means faster installs, fewer surprises, and a finish that holds up on Monday morning.

We're licensed, insured, and honest about what a job will take. If your timeline is tight, we'll tell you. If your quote needs to come down, we'll tell you what we can cut. No sales pressure, no upsells mid-project.

How we work

Four things we never cut corners on.

01 · Showing up

On time, every time.

Scheduled means scheduled. If something changes, you hear about it from us first — not from the crew waiting in the lobby.

02 · Protection

Your space, protected.

Floor runners, elevator pads, corner guards, shrink-wrap. Your walls and floors look the same when we leave as when we arrived.

03 · Precision

Planned to the hour.

Move plans that map every room, every piece, every handoff. Nothing shows up on the wrong floor.

04 · Finish

Ready for work.

We reset the space — power plugged in, chairs adjusted, monitors up — so your team can sit down and work.
